This screen allows you to add a lead to the system.

You will firstly need select and fill in all the details including country fro which the lead will be representing. Wether or not you have a budget approved. Contract time in which you would like the contract to be concluded. Lead time giving the DealLeader team a estimate on how long you want the lead prcess to be started within and lastly if you would like to inspect the products requested or not.

You will then click the add product button to select a product provided by the system.

Once completed please press the submit button and your details will be sent to a Deal Leader Executive to start the lead process.

If you are still unsure please click the Contact Us tab and contact us directly.
