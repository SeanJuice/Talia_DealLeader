This page allows you to see the lead details of your selected lead.

You can either click the send request for quote page button which will take you to the send request page allowing you to send a request for quote to all the suppliers who can supply the product on the lead. Or you can press the submit quote button which will allow you to submit a quote from the best request for quote recived back from the suppliers.

Clicking the return to leads button will take you back to the submitted leads page.
