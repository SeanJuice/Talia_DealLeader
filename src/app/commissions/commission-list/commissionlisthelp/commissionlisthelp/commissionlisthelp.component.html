This page shows you a list of all the commissions on the system currently.

Clicking the add commission button will take you to the add commission page. By clicking the download excel button it will download a exce spreadsheet for the you with all the commissions n the system. Clicking download PDF button will download a PDF of the current commissions for you.

By clicking the Update button you will be taken to the update commission page which will allow you to update the commission value. By clicking delete you will remove the commission value from the system.
