This page allows you to generate a report to give you a list of all the products finalized on the system.

You will need to select a start date and end date from the date pickers. For the time period you want to generate the report for.

By clicking the generate report button to generate a report with all the products finalized during that period entered on the system or by clicking the download PDF button to download a copy of all the produts finalized during the selcted period on the system.
